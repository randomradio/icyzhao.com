# Site Direction

## Role Of The Site

`icyzhao.com` should feel like a personal operating base:

- A place to publish ideas before they become polished essays.
- A durable archive of technical notes, projects, experiments, and writing.
- A navigation hub for subdomains and side projects under the same identity.
- A lightweight public profile that does not depend on social feeds.

## Content Types

| Type | Purpose | Default Length | URL Shape |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Note | Quick thoughts, fragments, observations | 1-5 paragraphs | `/notes/{slug}/` |
| Essay | More structured writing | Open-ended | `/writing/{slug}/` |
| Project | Built things, experiments, tools | Compact but maintained | `/projects/{slug}/` |
| Link | Saved references with commentary | Short | `/links/{slug}/` |
| Now | Current focus and status | Updated periodically | `/now/` |

## Homepage Shape

The homepage should answer three questions quickly:

1. Who is this?
2. What has been written or built recently?
3. Where else under this domain can I go?

Recommended sections:

- Recent writing and notes.
- Featured projects.
- Automatically generated domain map.
- A small profile block with durable contact links.

## Voice

The site should support both English and Chinese writing. Do not force every piece into the same tone. The organizing principle is clarity, not brand polish.

## Non-Goals For The First Pass

- No complex CMS.
- No comments system.
- No newsletter integration.
- No heavy design system before the publishing loop works.
